Our Trips Categories

Epic journeys

Make the journey from A to Z the essence of your trip. Hiking, biking, skiing and even customized vespa tours. Focus on growth, challenge and achievement.

Hidden tribes

Visit indigenous peoples that use tourism to support their traditional way of life. Sustainable tourism plays in important role in the tribe's self determination. Focus on community tourism.

Eco lodges & tours

Stay in lodging that puts nature, conservation and environmental protection at the forefront of their offering. Focus on sustainable tourism.

Endangered Wildlife

Visit and learn about wildlife that is considered “threatened” or higher by the International Union for Conservation of Nature. Focus on wildlife conservation.

Our Story

______________

One day, one jump and two years of passion

After jumping off the side of a cliff with only a cord attached in the Swiss Alps, our founder couldn't go back to his day job. Instead, he set out to create something that he couldn't find in the travel market - A collection of customizable adventure itineraries around the globe that are rewarding to the traveler, host and the environment. Pygmy Elephant is the end result of two years of full time travel in search of the best adventure experiences the world has to offer.

Our Service Categories

Consult & Book

We help you understand product offerings from our carefully selected supplier base. We book lodging and scheduled group tours as well as related transfers and logistics where necessary

Travel World Iteneraries

We create unique itineraries by combining lodging, transportation, equipment and experiences and handling complex logistics. These itineraries can be self guided or privately guided and can be tailored to suit your desires.

From Scratch

You bring us an idea and we turn it into reality. We draw your exclusive journey from scratch including complex logistics, experiences, guides and/or guidebooks, lodging, equipment, and itinerary. Tell us your epic idea, and will make it happen.

Our Pricing Guarantees

Best price guarantee

For products with a Best Price Guarantee, we will meet or beat any offer currently advertised on the internet. The offer must be for the same level of service and not connected with any conditional offerings.

Never lose your deposit

For Pygmy and From Scratch itineraries, if you must cancel for any reason not covered under insurance, we will waive the deposit on any future Pygmy or From Scratch Itinerary of equal value.

Best price guarantee

With private tour production, it is almost unheard of to provide a price breakdown of lodging, guides, transfers, equipment and fees. We don’t think that is fair to the traveler. With our pricing transparency, you will see an itemized breakdown of costs. When we work with local partners who organize the bulk of the trip, we estimate costs to help you understand where your money is going.
